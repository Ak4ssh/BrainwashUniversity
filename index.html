Tapping talent

We leave no stone unturned in honing talent beyond the classroom. Brainware University students actively engage in a host of club activities.

The project Celebrating Tagore is an initiative spearheaded by Brainware University's Chancellor, Phalguni Mookhopadhayay, attributed to Rabindranath Tagore, the eminent poet, philosopher, and Nobel laureate. Mr. Mookhopadhayay aims to sing over 100 songs composed by Tagore. The project also aims to honour and propagate Tagore's literary and cultural contributions among the non-bengali speaking population of the country along with abroad. It seeks to disseminate Tagore's philosophy, creativity, and vision to inspire and enrich the lives of individuals, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of his timeless legacy.
